Essere means 'to be'. Essere is an irregular verb as it is different between tenses. The conjugation is also irregular between tenses.

| English     | Present    |
| ----------- | ---------- |
| I am        | Io sono    |
| You are     | Tu sei     |
| You are     | Lei e      |
| He/She      | Lui/Lei  e |
| We are      | Noi siamo  |
| You all are | Voi siete  |
| They are    | Loro sono  |
